Diffusion

Diffusion tracks summarisation topics across a time series of reports. The diagram below is a representations of a Boolean valued topic-report matrix for the agreement Revitalised Agreement on the Resolution of the Conflict in the Republic of South Sudan (R-ARCSS). Summarisation topics are arranged in order of first appearance in the report sequence. The matrix provides information about:

  • Which summarisation topics are mentioned most.
  • Which reports mention the most summarisation topics.
  • When a summarisation topic first appears in the report sequence.

In the diagram below a dot represents a report containing a summarisation topics.
