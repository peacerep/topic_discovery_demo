Diffusion

Diffusion tracks summarisation topics across a time series of reports. The diagrams below are representations of a Boolean valued topic-report matrix for the agreement Revitalised Agreement on the Resolution of the Conflict in the Republic of South Sudan (R-ARCSS). Summarisation topics are arranged in order of first appearance in the report sequence. The matrix provides information about:

  • Which summarisation topics are mentioned most (row marginals).
  • Which reports mention the most summarisation topics (column marginals).
  • When a summarisation topic first appears in the report sequence.
  • Clustering of summarisation topics.

The y-axis contains the 162 summarisation topics, and the x-axis the 68 implementation reports that follow the agreement. Reports are in date order. In the first diagram the cell values are:

  • 0 coded as black. The report in the column does not contain the summarisation topic in the row.
  • 1 coded as yellow. The report in the column does contain the summarisation topic in the row.
In the second diagram a dot represents a report containing a summarisation topics.

The report at report index 7 is the JMEC-1st-Qtr-2020-Report-FINAL 1 which contains 81 of the possible 162 topics.
