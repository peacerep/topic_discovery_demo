Topic Discovery and Summarisation

In this analysis we find the topics common to a single agreement and a set of implementation reports that follow the agreement. The example uses the Revitalised Agreement on the Resolution of the Conflict in the Republic of South Sudan (R-ARCSS)< and the 66 implementation reports that follow it.

Topic listing

Below are 162 summarisation topics that the Revitalised Agreement on the Resolution of the Conflict in the Republic of South Sudan (R-ARCSS) has in common with the 68 implementation reports that follow it. Implementation reports include JMEC, RJMEC, and CTSAMVM reports.

A summarisation topic is represented by a pair of text segments comprising an agreement provision and a report section. Each pair is generated by the cluster analysis process described on the Introduction page. The topics are in the order they appear in the report sequence.
