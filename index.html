Topic Discovery

Describe the process here.

A degree centrality algorithm is applied to all 65 clusters to identify the connections between the nodes of the network. This summarisation process analyses a cluster to identify the section and the provision that have the most connections to other provisions and sections, respectively. These sentences summarise the content of the cluster. This summary pair can be thought of as a topic in the document and are referred to here as summarisation topics. Each cluster produces a summarisation topic.
